About Srinagar

Srinagar, the summer capital of Jammu and Kashmir, is renowned for its stunning natural beauty, serene lakes, and Mughal gardens. Located in the picturesque Kashmir Valley, Srinagar is surrounded by the Himalayas and is famous for its houseboats, shikara rides on Dal Lake, and traditional Kashmiri handicrafts. The city's rich cultural heritage and breathtaking landscapes make it a popular destination for tourists seeking tranquility and adventure.
