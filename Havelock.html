About Havelock

Havelock Island, part of the Andaman and Nicobar Islands, is renowned for its pristine beaches, lush greenery, and vibrant coral reefs. Popular for activities such as scuba diving, snorkeling, and sea-walking, Havelock offers visitors an opportunity to explore its rich marine biodiversity and stunning underwater landscapes. With its tranquil atmosphere and breathtaking sunsets, Havelock Island is a paradise for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts alike.
