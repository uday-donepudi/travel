About Alleppey

Alleppey, also known as Alappuzha, is a charming town located in the southern Indian state of Kerala. Renowned for its picturesque backwaters, tranquil beaches, and vibrant houseboat cruises, Alleppey offers visitors a unique blend of natural beauty and cultural experiences. The town is famous for its annual Nehru Trophy Boat Race, held on the Punnamada Lake, where beautifully decorated traditional snake boats compete for glory. With its serene waterways, lush paddy fields, and delicious seafood, Alleppey is a must-visit destination for travelers seeking relaxation and adventure.
