About Leh

Lakshadweep, meaning 'a hundred thousand islands' in Sanskrit, is an archipelago situated off the southwestern coast of India in the Laccadive Sea. Renowned for its pristine coral reefs and crystal-clear waters, Lakshadweep is a paradise for divers and beach enthusiasts alike. Its serene beaches, lush greenery, and vibrant marine life make it a perfect destination for relaxation and exploration.
