About Rameshwaram

Rameshwaram, on Pamban Island in Tamil Nadu, is a revered Hindu pilgrimage site known for its ancient temples, sacred water tanks, and beautiful beaches. Legend has it as the place where Lord Rama built a bridge to Lanka in the Ramayana. The Ramanathaswamy Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, is a major attraction with its stunning architecture and sacred rituals. With its spiritual significance and peaceful ambiance, Rameshwaram draws devotees and tourists alike seeking enlightenment and tranquility.
