About Coorg

Coorg, also known as Kodagu, is a serene hill station located in the Western Ghats of Karnataka, India. Renowned for its coffee plantations, mist-covered hills, and lush greenery, Coorg is often referred to as the "Scotland of India." The region is home to several attractions, including the Abbey Falls, Dubare Elephant Camp, and Talakaveri, the source of the river Kaveri. With its pleasant climate, beautiful landscapes, and rich cultural heritage, Coorg is a popular destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.
