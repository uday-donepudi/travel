About Puri

Puri, located in the eastern Indian state of Odisha, is a vibrant coastal city famous for its rich heritage and religious significance. It is home to the renowned Jagannath Temple, dedicated to Lord Jagannath, which attracts millions of devotees and tourists annually. Puri is also known for its pristine beaches, such as the Puri Beach and Konark Beach, where visitors can relax and enjoy water sports. The city hosts the famous Rath Yatra festival, during which the idols of Lord Jagannath.
