About Amritsar

Amritsar, located in the northwestern Indian state of Punjab, is a city with a rich cultural and religious heritage. The city is home to the iconic Golden Temple, the holiest shrine in Sikhism, which attracts millions of pilgrims and tourists every year. Amritsar is also famous for its delicious street food, vibrant markets, and historical landmarks such as the Jallianwala Bagh and the Wagah Border. With its warm hospitality and spiritual atmosphere, Amritsar offers visitors a memorable experience and a glimpse into the rich tapestry of Indian culture.
